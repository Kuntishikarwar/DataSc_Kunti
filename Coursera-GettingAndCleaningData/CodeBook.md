This codebook defines the features/attributes of final_tidy_data.txt derived after performing multiple cleansing operations on set of raw data obtained from experiments that have been carried out with a group of 30 volunteers each performing six activities (WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING)wearing a smartphone .

**************Identifiers*******************
subject:  ID of the subject on whome the experiment was performed
activityname: The Activity performed by the test subejct(WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING)
